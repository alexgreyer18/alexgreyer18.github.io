What they don't tell you

Trading is a marathon, not a sprint.

It is not all big gains and glory. Many will try to show you how profitable they are with big lots while using demo accounts. Using bigger lots when you are new to trading is quite dangerous. You are risking more money than you have by using "leverage" which means that your losses are proportional to yours gains and since you don't know what you are doing yet, they can wipe you out in an instant.

That is not reliable over time. Overleveraging is one of the many ways to blow your account.
